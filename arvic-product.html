Arvic Product

What's Arvic?

Arvic is a digital tool that enables individuals to track and manage their health data in a secure and accessible manner. Arvic is often designed to be user-friendly and may include features such as reminders for medication or appointments, the ability to set health goals and track progress, and access to health-related resources and information.

What we can provide?

Arvic has many building block components that allow users to store and organize information such as their medical history, telemedicine, health assessment, and other health-related data. With a Health Wallet, users can have complete control over their health information, allowing them to update and share it with their healthcare providers as needed.

Health Drive

Arvic has a health drives feature that allows users to keep track of their health history from various sources, including self-inputted data, hospitals, clinics, labs, and wearable devices. In addition, users can easily collect and store their health data, which can be shared with their healthcare provider for further analysis and diagnostics.

Health Assessment

Arvic's Health assessment provides valuable information that helps identify potential health risks, detect early signs of diseases, and develop preventative strategies to manage these risks. By detecting and managing potential health risks early on, healthcare providers can improve health outcomes and enhance the quality of life for their patients.

Symptom Checker

Arvic has a symptom checker that is proven by med-school is a set of diagnostic criteria that have been developed and validated by medical professionals, typically based on established medical research and standards of practice. These diagnostic criteria are used to identify common symptoms and signs that may be indicative of certain medical conditions or diseases.

Telemedicine

Arvic's Telemedicine is delivering healthcare services remotely using telecommunications technology, such as video conferencing, phone calls, or secure messaging. With telemedicine, users can receive medical advice, diagnosis, and treatment without needing to visit a physical clinic or hospital, making it a convenient and accessible option for our users.
