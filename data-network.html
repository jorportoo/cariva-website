Data Network

Data Connectivity Made Easy

Connect with multiple devices and partners effortlessly

Highlights the ease and convenience of using a data network to connect with multiple sources in healthcare & wellness industries. With Cariva's data network provides a seamlessly collaborate and communicate with different devices and partners, including hospitals, labs, pharmacies, IoT, medical hardware and wearable devices without any hassle or complications.

Hospital & Labs

Connecting through all partners. Our data collaboration solutions are designed to comply with all regulatory requirements, ensuring data security and patient confidentiality.

Medical Hardware

Offering a range of medical hardware that enables the diagnosis of vital signs such as blood pressure, blood sugar, body temperature, and SpO2. These medical devices are seamlessly integrated with our data network, allowing for real-time transfer of patient data to our centralized platform.

IoT & Wearable Device

Simplify the process of connecting to IoT and wearable devices, ensuring easy and secure data transfer. This approach enables healthcare providers to monitor patient health remotely, providing personalized treatment recommendations and identifying trends or anomalies in patient health.

Pharmacy

Connecting with over 600 partner pharmacies, enabling users to access telemedicine and telepharmacy services all in one place. This approach streamlines healthcare delivery, improves patient outcomes, and complies with all regulatory requirements for patient confidentiality and data security.
