NextCercise Product

What's NextCercise?

NextCercise was founded by Cariva (Thailand) Company Limited with the aim of becoming a top health application that motivates people to maintain a healthy lifestyle through enjoyable and effortless methods. The application is tailored to suit all lifestyles, whether you work from home, stay indoors, or exercise regularly. Our primary objective is to help everyone improve their physical fitness and reap the rewards.

Make Every Move Count!

What we can provide?

NextCercise has many building block components that enhance user experiences and engagement, while also being simple to integrate, manage, and maintain. Moreover, NextCercise's block components are consistently up-to-date, with a lower Time-to-Value and reduced costs of development and implementation.

IOS/Android data connection

NextCercise is available to download from both iOS and Android to connect the data. iOS can sync the data with Apple Health and Andriod can sync the data with Google Fit.

Leaderbaord

Leaderboard is a feature to track and display the performance of individuals or teams in an activity, Challenge. It can increase motivation, engagement, and user performance.

Challenge System

The Challenge system is an efficient feature that encourages individuals or teams to set goals, promotes accountability and teamwork, and fosters learning and development. Additionally, rewards can be used to further enhance the challenge.

Earn point / coin system

NextCercise Once a user's movement has been tracked, and they have earned coins, they can redeem rewards through the app. There are numerous ways to incentivize users to engage in physical activity to improve their health. Users can exchange their HP for coins and use them to claim rewards. The rewards vary in price, ranging from 50 coins to over 200,000 coins.
